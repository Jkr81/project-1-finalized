Rocky

French bulldog

Rocky is a male french bulldog who loves to play and run around. He will jump and play with anyone that approaches him and will trade his owners over a treat. He is loving and funny to be around.

About Rocky

Rocky loves to sleep all day and jump on the funiture when no one is looking. After every meal, he heads to the front of the couch and starts to roll and run around the house. He might be crazy but is adorable in the process.

Favorite activities

Rocky loves to walk around the lake for about 20 minutes and interact with his friends along the way. He enjoys chasing down squirrels and runing after ducks. And when in an extra playful mood, he runs away from his owners as they try to catch him. In a family of 5, he is the main attraction.
